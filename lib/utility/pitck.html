Company Overview

Samay is a super app that simplifies booking appointments for various services, including:

  • Doctors
  • Salons & Spas
  • Gyms & Yoga
  • Job Searches
  • Tattoo Artists
  • Events
  • Schools
  • Hotels & Restaurants

Additionally, Samay provides service providers with tools to manage appointments, calendars, sales, business reports, product sales, and job postings.

PROBLEM

For Customers

Time Waste

Have you been waiting in long lines while visiting the doctor, salon, restaurants, or any other places?

Platform Fragmentation

There is no centralized platform to book different types of appointments. Waste of time switching from one platform to another.

Service Uncertainty

Customers are not familiar with the services and prices offered in different categories.

Quality Uncertainty

There is no credible way to know the quality of a service provider's business.

PROBLEM

For Businesses

  • Many businesses still use pen and paper and Microsoft Excel to manage customer appointments

  • Traditional phone or WhatsApp for appointment taking. The phone number is not always publicly available for customers

  • There is no way to analyze business reports such as employee, sales, and customer data

  • Getting new users is hard, and there's no centralized system for tracking business operations

  • For businesses, it is very difficult to hire employees and send personalized promotions

Medical Section

Problems

  • Patients and family members must wait long to visit a doctor.

  • The doctor's prescription is difficult to read.

  • Doctors are not aware of the patient's medical history like allergies, previous major diseases, or genetic conditions.

  • Patients are often unaware of the costs associated with medical checkups, lab tests, and daily hospital charges.

Solutions

  • Easy appointment booking with top doctors with just a few clicks on your mobile phone.

  • Digital menu displays prices for doctor visits, lab tests, and other hospital services.

  • Timeline feature displays past medical history on a user's profile.

  • Medical history storage assists doctors in understanding health backgrounds, saving time and improving care.
